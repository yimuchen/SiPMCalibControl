Detailed Description

Commands that are used for looking up, displaying and modifying session parameters. This includes loading and saving the master coordinate calibration results, setting the readout devices, and commands used to stall the program. Notice that "setting the readout devices" will only handle the opening and closing of device interfaces, not the actual readout device internal settings. That should be handled in their designate command files.
